Introduction

The Islamic Center of Fayetteville (ICF) considers one of our primary purposes to be to help both Muslims and non-Muslims develop or improve their understanding of Islam. In today’s world, misunderstanding Islam seems to be at the core of many problems affecting Muslims and non-Muslims around the world, and we believe that education and improved understanding may go a long way toward reducing or eliminating such problems. Therefore, should you have any questions that we may be able to address to help improve your understanding of this great way of life, please contact us by clicking the contact us botton or by email at islamcentfay@yahoo.com.We are also available to speak with your group or to meet with you individually to discuss this topic.
